About Me

Hi, I’m Natheesan, a passionate frontend developer and a second-year Computer Science undergraduate at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Sri Lanka. I come from Jaffna, and I’ve always had a deep interest in coding, design, and building user-friendly web experiences. Academically, I’ve been a strong performer throughout. I completed my G.C.E. Ordinary Level with 9 A passes, and went on to achieve A2B in Advanced Level Mathematics, specializing in Combined Maths. My academic success led me to pursue my dream of studying Computer Science, and I’m currently working towards my BComp (Hons) degree, expected to graduate in 2028. In my first year, I learned core programming languages like C++ and Java, and gained practical experience working with MySQL databases. These fundamentals helped me understand problem-solving, algorithms, and data management. Now in my second year, I’m focusing on Web Technologies. I’ve been working with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, building responsive and visually appealing websites. I particularly enjoy the creativity and logic involved in frontend development, where I get to turn ideas into real, interactive designs. I love experimenting with new design trends and improving my coding skills through hands-on projects. I enjoy both working independently and collaborating with others, especially when it involves UI design and frontend integration. My goal is to become a skilled frontend developer who builds meaningful digital products that make a positive impact. I believe learning is a continuous journey, and I’m excited to grow, explore new technologies, and contribute to the world through code. Thanks for stopping by my portfolio. Let’s connect and create something awesome!
